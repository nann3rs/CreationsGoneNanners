It all started with a...

...gift!
I began my journey creating coasters as a Christmas gift for all my cousins. Disney has always been a big part of our lives and every Christmas I tried incorporating that into their presents. After last year's Christmas gift giving was a success, I wanted to be able to bring that same joy from my home to others. Creating these magical coasters has been so rewarding! Seeing others share their excitement of my craft, I feel accomplished in being able to bring that same happiness to their home.
